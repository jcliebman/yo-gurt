Soccer Duel

Download
Soccer Duel, a 1v1 soccer game created by Felix Figueroa. Heavy players tend to win the ball in duels, and they have the best shooting stats. Light players are crazy fast, but they are weak and have poor shooting. Normal players are average, running and shooting well, but they have no special advantage. Ensure that python 3 is installed on your device.
Option 1:
Unzip soccergame.zip and then open README.txt. Follow the instructions to play. The device must use bash.
Option 2:
Unzip soccergame.zip. You can now run game.py anyway you wish.
